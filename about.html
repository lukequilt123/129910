About us

A partnership of volunteers and scientists.

We are a registered charity working to secure the future for birds and nature — using our science, monitoring and data to inform good environmental decisions, and inspiring others with the wonder of birds.

How we work

Tens of thousands of volunteers. A core of professional scientists. One shared mission.

For more than ninety years, BTO has combined the passion of birdwatchers with rigorous, independent science. Our surveys are among the longest-running biological datasets in the world — and that depth is exactly what makes them powerful.

We don't campaign. We provide the trusted evidence that everyone else relies on, freely and impartially.

What guides us

Our values

01

Evidence first

Robust, independent data underpins everything. We follow the science wherever it leads.

02

Powered by people

Our volunteers are the heart of BTO — their observations make the impossible possible.

03

Open & impartial

We share our findings widely so that good decisions can be made for nature.

04

Becoming more inclusive

We are committed to breaking down the barriers that keep people from nature and from us.
